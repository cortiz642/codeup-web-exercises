10 FAQs About National Parks

1. First President to start the National Park Act
President Ulysses Grant signed the Yellowstone National Park Protection Act into law on March 1, 1872.
2. Do you know where three of the 10 highest waterfalls in the world are located??
Three of the 10 tallest waterfalls are located in Yosemite National Park in California.
3. Where is the longest cave system located?
The longest cave system in the world is called Mammoth Cave National Park in Kentucky, which is 3,454 mapped miles.
4. Are there any Super Volcano's within the US?
Yes! The Yellowstone Caldera, Yellowstone National Park is a super volcano that is responsible for three of the world's six biggest volcano eruptions.
5. Where is the US is the most remote national park?
The Dry Tortugas National Park located 70 miles from the Key West in the Gulf of Mexico is a collection of seven tiny islands that can be reached only by boat or plane.
